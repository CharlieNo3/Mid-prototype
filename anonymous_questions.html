How to handle communication barriers with clients?

Sent to: Marketing Mentor Sent yesterday

Hello, I've encountered some barriers when communicating with clients. When clients ask many technical questions, I'm not sure how to respond in a way that demonstrates professionalism without over-promising. Any suggestions?

Yesterday 15:30
Marketing Mentor

That's a great question! When communicating with clients, encountering technical questions you're uncertain about is quite common. I suggest:

  1. Honesty is the best policy. If you're unsure, say "That's a good question, let me verify the specific details and get back to you with a precise answer."
  2. Prepare some general response templates for common questions
  3. Learn to use the "bridging" technique to guide the question to areas you're more familiar with
  4. Regularly discuss common client questions with team members and share experiences
